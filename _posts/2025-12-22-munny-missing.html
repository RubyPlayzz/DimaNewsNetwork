This morning, resident of Rising City "Munny" has been officially delclared missing by the Jailbreak Police Force.

Who was Munny?

Munny was a resident of the Jailbreak Rising City and a member of the police force. She had many friends and was a well respected member of the community.

Munny was met by RubyPlayz64 along with his team (VexorgIRL & BloxBunig) while they were recording a video.


VexorgIRL and BloxBunig were recording a video together where they would say "We are not horses, we are humans, and humans are ..." before proceeding to jump off of the tallest tower in Rising City after saying. Witnesses say this was to pay respects to their friend Seong Gi-hun who had sacrificed his life after saying the same thing.


Munny, being a member of the Jailbreak police force, courageously climbed the tall building they were recording on as they were illegally there and could have been badly injured if the stunt went wrong.


After they explained what they were doing, Munny was understanding but still told them they would have to stop or be detained.


While VexorgIRL and RubyPlayz64 were understanding, BloxBunig was outraged that Munny was stopping them from paying respects to their friend.

He got the group & Munny to enter their helicopter, before ejecting Munny from her seat while they were flying directly above a dormant volcano.

While Munny survived the incident, she was left with many severe burns.

When the team (including BloxBunig) apologised, Munny accepted their apology, showing her respect to the team and forgiveness.


After the incident, Munny and the team became great friends and assisted each other with their jobs, Munny helping with content creation and the team helping her with her policing duties.


Unfortunately, the story doesn't have a happy ending as Munny cut ties with RubyPlayz64, BloxBunig and VexorgIRL after their serious criminal activities and charges, including the raid of Yuvin Marasini's Mafiaton.

Where is she?

As of now Munny's whereabouts are unknown.

However, there are many theories on what happened to her.


Images have been resurfacing of Munny seen at Yuvin Marasini's Mafiaton. As she was a member of the Jailbreak Police force, it is possible that Munny was the whistleblower that eventually lead to it's raid and arrest of Yuvin.


While police are still investigating, it is possible that Munny's was kidnapped by connections of Kendy D, Big Nas Y, and RubyPlayz64.


A noteable fact about Munny's disappearance is that, while she cut ties with VexorgIRL and BloxBunig, she remained a connection of RubyPlayz64 for many months after. Many are speculating that Munny did this so she could learn more about RubyPlayz64 as she knew he how dangerous of a criminal he was compared to BloxBunig and VexorgIRL. This act of selflessness by Munny may have also lead to her kidnapping.


Another theory is that Munny has simply left the Jailbreak Island, however her online profiles still list her as a member of Badimo so it's unlikely she did.

Conclusion

While this was likely a targeted attack, Jailbreak police still advice everyone in Rising City to be extra alert and do not engage with any gangs. If you are a resident of Rising City and believe you are in danger please go to your nearest police station and help will be provided.


If you have any information about Munny, please contact the nearest jailbreak police station.


Jailbreak is mourning the loss of Munny.
